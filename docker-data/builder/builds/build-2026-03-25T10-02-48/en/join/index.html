Frequently Asked Questions

INSLAB focuses on three pillars: DATA (data collection and processing), SYSTEM (intelligent networks and Edge AI), and SERVICE (IoT applications and solutions). With intent-based autonomous intelligent systems as our core direction, you can explore topics across these areas based on your interests.

Yes, graduate students are supported through research project funding, university scholarships, and teaching assistantships. Details are discussed during the admission process.
